About

This site was made as an assigment for a web development course. The prompt for this assignment was to make a website about a mythical creature using HTML and CSS.

My site is inspired by a time--an embarrasingly recent time--that I truly believed mermaids were real due to a very convincing mockumentary that I thought was a documentary.
